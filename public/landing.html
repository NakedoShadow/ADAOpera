A full symphonic ensemble — strings, woodwinds, brass, percussion, keyboards — all synthesized in the browser. Vivaldi AI conducts: auto-orchestration, Four Seasons presets, velocity curves, bowing expression engine. Play classical music with feeling that rivals hardware.
